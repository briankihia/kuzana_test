Travel Policy

This document outlines the company's travel policy for approved business travel.

Guidelines:
- All travel requires manager approval
- Use company preferred providers when available
- Expense reporting due within 5 business days
- Keep all receipts for reimbursement

Categories:
- Flights: Book 2+ weeks in advance for discounts
- Hotels: Choose hotels within per-diem limits
- Ground Transportation: Use rideshare or rental as needed
- Meals: Standard meal allowance per location
